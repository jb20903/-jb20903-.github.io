The Lacrosse Life

Lacrosse is an up and coming sport that youth across the world are being introduced to. Lacrosse is thought to have originated with various tribes of Native Americans . Over the years, the sport and the equipment have evolved as different immigrants have come to America and molded the sport into what it is today. When I was in high school, I played lacrosse and had a lacrosse coach that had played the sport professionally before he began coaching. Women’s lacrosse varies greatly from men’s lacrosse. My coach always used to joke that they attempted to water down the game of lacrosse for women to play, but the women playing just became more intellectual on how they approached the play of the game. Men’s lacrosse is aimed to be a more physical game where the players where shoulder pads and helmets and are permitted to hit players of the opposite team with their stick. Women’s lacrosse is designed to be played more strategically as the players only wear goggles and a mouth-piece and are not allowed to explicitly hit their opponents. The sticks are also structured differently from the men’s lacrosse sticks as the men’s lacrosse sticks are built with a pocket and require less cradling and women’s sticks are the opposite.

Lacrosse is similar to a few other sports played in America such as football and soccer. The lacrosse field can also be utilized as a soccer field and a football field. On the field there are 11 players in addition to the goalie. The positions of lacrosse players are attack, defense, midfield, and goalie. Attack is responsible for scoring points in the game, they have strong stick skills and are able to pass and throw the ball effectively and efficiently. Defense is responsible for protecting the goalie as much as possible and defending the goal from attackers trying to score. Midfielders play the role of both attack and defense and are required to run the entire field and be able to move the ball effectively from one end of the field to the other. The goalie like in other sports is responsible for keeping the goal and preventing other people from scoring; in addition to this the goalie is responsible for clearing the ball back down the field after they make a save. Unlike many other sports, the goalie in lacrosse is able to leave the goal and be a participant in the game; doing this however puts a greater risk on the goalie that the other team may score while they are not occupying the goal.

A sport that has been around longer than a lot of others, yet still has not gained the popularity and following of many newer sports. Lacrosse was up and coming when I was playing in high school especially in my area. Generally, lacrosse is much more popular at high schools and colleges in more northern states and those near larger major cities. As the sport begins to grow its teams, the fanbase grows along with it. To learn more about lacrosse across the country, visit The US Lacrosse Website.
